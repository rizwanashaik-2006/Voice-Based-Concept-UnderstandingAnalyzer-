

# Problem Solution Fit Canvas

## 1. Customer Segment (CS)

* Undergraduate and postgraduate students
* Competitive exam aspirants
* Teachers and faculty members
* Training institutes and educational organizations
* Candidates preparing for interviews and presentations

---

## 2. Problems / Pain Points (PR)

* Difficulty in assessing conceptual understanding through verbal explanations.
* Traditional exams focus mainly on written answers.
* Students often memorize concepts without understanding them deeply.
* Teachers require significant time to evaluate oral responses manually.

---

## 3. Triggers to Act (TR)

* Preparation for viva examinations and interviews.
* Need for instant feedback on conceptual understanding.
* Desire to improve communication and presentation skills.
* Requirement for self-assessment outside the classroom.

---

## 4. Emotions (EM)

### Before:

* Nervousness
* Self-doubt
* Fear of poor performance

### After:

* Increased confidence
* Motivation to improve
* Better understanding of strengths and weaknesses

---

## 5. Available Solutions (AS)

* Written examinations
* Online quizzes and MCQ platforms
* Manual oral evaluations by teachers
* LMS platforms with limited verbal assessment capabilities

---

## 6. Customer Limitations (CL)

* Limited access to personalized feedback.
* Difficulty identifying weak conceptual areas.
* Lack of tools for evaluating spoken explanations.
* Variations in microphone and internet quality.

---

## 7. User Behavior (BE)

* Students often rely on rote learning.
* Learners practice concepts individually without evaluation.
* Most students receive feedback only during examinations.
* Verbal communication skills are rarely measured.

---

## 8. Channels of Distribution (CH)

### Online:

* Web application
* College LMS platforms
* Cloud deployment platforms

### Offline:

* Classroom demonstrations
* Lab sessions
* Workshops and training programs

---

## 9. Problem Root Cause (RC)

* Existing educational systems prioritize written assessments.
* Limited availability of AI-powered verbal evaluation tools.
* Lack of scalable methods for personalized oral feedback.
* Communication and conceptual understanding are assessed separately.

---

## 10. Your Solution (SL)

An AI-powered application that:

* Accepts spoken explanations from users.
* Converts speech into text using speech recognition.
* Evaluates conceptual understanding using semantic similarity techniques.
* Analyzes fluency, pauses, and filler words.
* Generates scores, recommendations, and downloadable reports for improvement.
